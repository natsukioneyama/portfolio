NATSUKI ONEYAMA MAKE-UP ARTIST

BIOGRAPHY

Natsuki Oneyama is a Paris-based makeup artist working across editorial, advertising and e-commerce.

Born in Japan and now based in France, he developed his career in Paris after assisting on major editorials and backstage at fashion shows. He worked for four years as First Assistant to Marie Duhart at Bryant Artists before becoming independent.

His work focuses on clean, modern beauty with a strong attention to skin, texture and natural light. Alongside makeup, he provides hair grooming and on-set beauty support, ensuring consistency and precision throughout the shoot.

He collaborates with photographers, brands and creative teams on studio and location projects, with a particular focus on e-commerce, advertising and digital content.

Fluent in both Japanese and French environments, he works across international productions in Paris.
